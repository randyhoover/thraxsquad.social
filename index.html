About Me

Hi, I'm Randy aka Thrax or Thraxsquad. I own a 2022 Tesla Model 3 Performance in Orlando Florida. I've always been passionate about cars, and I'm grateful I'm at that point in life where I'm able to build something that I think is truly unique and different from what you currently see driving around.

Besides just working on my car, attending car meets and car shows I also own and run Electrified Car Club which is a car club for exclusively electric vehicles. We host meets, rallies, shows, and also host events to give back to the community, which ultimately is my favorite part.

I am also a member of Ruined EV. Ruined EV is the industry leading team in EV Modifications. Partnering with many top brands, and events, and continuing to push the limits of what is possible, and imagined for EV's.

Electrified Care

I have chosen to partner with Electrified Garage and their Electrified Care program, to allow me to retain my peace of mind with my battery, drive unit, and air conditioning. It allows me to maintain an extended warranty on these components even though at this point Tesla would not touch my vehicle. The team at Electrified Garage are my most trusted shop for the crucial components of my car and I couldn't be happier to partner with them on this.
